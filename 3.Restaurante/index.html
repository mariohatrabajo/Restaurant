The best restaurant in Asia

Karbranth is celebrating another year, this time with the Silphrena Restaurant, by chef Dalinar Kholin. The first hanoian restaurant to obtain 3 Michelin Stars has been considered this year’s best restaurant of Asia by the The World’s 50 Best.

Our journey

Our story begins as every story, with love. Gavilar and Navani Kholin got married on october of 1989.

Gavilar Kholin had a few years of experiences on restaurants of all kind and with Navani’s knowledge of numbers they decided to open their own restaurant in Hanói. They called it Sylphrena.

After the premature death of Gavilar, the manadgement of the restaurant reached the hands of his daughter, Jasnah Kholin, who named her uncle and brother of Gavilar, Dalinar Kholin, the chef of Sylphrena.

With all those years of history there came also a lot of new recipes, including dishes from all over the world prepared with the most care and meticulousness.

Sylphrena has experienced the visits of thousands of clients from all over the world, as well as various food critics. All of them exited the local with a smile on their faces.

This and much more is what have granted Sylphrena 3 Michelin Stars and 2 Repsol Suns, but Dalinar Kholin is an ambitious man and ensures that ‘The most important words a man can say are: “I will do better.”’.

Today, Sylphrena has become one of the best restaurants in Asia and in the world thanks to the dedication the Kholin family has put in the project.
