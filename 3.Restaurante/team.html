Our team

Dalinar Kholin

Our chef

Dalinar Kholin has the honor to be the current chef of the famous restaurant Sylphrena, founded by his brother now more than 30 years ago.

In 2003, Dalinar became Sylphrena’s chef after the death of his brother and Jasnah becoming the restaurant manager. Dalinar offered himself for the position because he wanted to make Gavilar proud.

Our manager

Jasnah Kholin, daughter of Gavilar and Navani Kholin, founders of Sylphrena, has been since, the premature death of Gavilar in 2003, our manager.

She has proved herself worthy of the restaurant of his parents guiding our team through tough times with diligence and calculated efficiency.

Our investors always say she comes accross as the type of person you could rely on to make smart business decissions and that's why they support our Restaurant.

Jasnah Kholin

The kitchen

The heart of our team of professionals is, of course, on the kitchen. Accompaning chef Dalinar are 4 cooks with lots of experience in all kinds of restaurants and dishes. We believe we must aid future generations in their professional careers. That’s why we include 3 cooking students as kitchen assistants.

Kaladin, Vin and Velo

The team

The rest of our team consists of waiters and accountants.

To deliver our delicious food to our customers, we trust on our efficient 3 waiters, Kaladin, Vin and Velo. They have accompanied us for now 5 years.

Helping Jasnah with the managing of Sylphrena are 2 accountants, Shallan and Tindwyl. Jasnah sometimes ask Navani, her mother and previous manager of Sylphrena, for advice. ‘We must respect the knowledge of older generations’ says Jasnah.
